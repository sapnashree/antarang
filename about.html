From Sketch to Reality,
Designing Your Tomorrow

At ANTARANG DESIGN STUDIO, we believe that interior designing is an art, demanding the touch of innovative, creative, matured, and nurtured minds to make homes beautifully elegant and aesthetically appealing. Established in 2010, our experienced team has a proven track record of surpassing customer expectations in residential and corporate sectors across Maharashtra. With a decade of service, our evolving and inspiring designs reflect a commitment to understanding customer requirements, blending creativity with modern trends, and prioritizing quality output. Our aim is to breathe soul into every customer's property, building a portfolio of satisfied clients.

OUR MISSION


Customer satisfaction takes priority ahead of all other considerations, and we aim to go beyond customer expectations in every way. To be recognised as the most renowned interior design firm operating within the area.

OUR Vision

Through our design practise, we are creating new places and giving them new souls. We aim to create spaces that are not just useful but also evocative via the design work that we do.

Director

Guiding Visions, Shaping Success: Where Leadership Meets Innovation.

Passion towards creativity and always striving to do things differently has made him step into the world of design. With specialized experience in new residential construction, renovation projects, and custom furniture, textiles, and interior lighting, Jayant Arwat makes every design project truly unique for his clients. He received his MBA in Project Management and Interior Designing from MIT College, Pune. He has undertaken several high-end residential and corporate design projects for the firm. With over 12+ years of experience in luxury interior design, principal designer Mr. Jayant Arwat’s firm has catered various clients pan India through Antarang Design Studio and still continues to express philosophy of customizing design solutions that reflect the lifestyle and individuality of the clients.
